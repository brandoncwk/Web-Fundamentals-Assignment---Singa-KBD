Where It All Began


It all started with a YouTube video back in 2016. I hadn’t seen or heard anything like it - a custom mechanical keyboard. Honestly, it ruined prebuilt keyboards for me forever haha.

From then on, I was obsessed - I wanted to know more. Unfortunately, the Malaysian mechanical keyboard scene at the time was pretty small and there wasn’t much going on. It was around that time that I met Yutski, and he suggested something crazy: why not build your own keyboards?

How It Came to Be


Yutski taught me everything I knew about designing keyboards, and eventually, after much trial and error, I launched my first group buy under the SingaKBD brand, and things only picked up after that.

It’s been a few years now, and I’m still blown away that so many of you like my keyboards. From the bottom of my heart, thank you al! You’re the reason all of this has been possible!
