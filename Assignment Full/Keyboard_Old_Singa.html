The Singa

The King

Crafted with love, our debut board set the standard for Singa’s singnature traits of quality materials and unwavering craftmanship. Avaulable in a variety of colorways including frosted polycarbonate for fans of RGB.

Specifications

  • 75% Gasket
  • Aluminium or Copper plate
  • Anodized 6063 Aluminum or Sandblasted Polycarbonate
  • Available in Blue, Red, Ruby, Black, Salmon, and Semi-Translucent
  • 8 Degree angle
  • Average build weighs: approx 1800g
  • 1.5 mm plates in Black Aluminum/Polycarbonate
  • Acrylic slide in cover
  • Hiney PCB
