Answers to FAQs


Shipping fees are normalized for each group buy and are bundled in together with your purchases. All orders will ship via DHL Express. However, if prefer not to use DHL, do reach out to us and we’ll arrange for alternative shipping with a courier of your choice.

We ship worldwide but if your country isn’t listed during check out please reach out to us for assistance.

Orders are processed and shipped within 2-3 business days. However, if we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

Shipment confirmation and order tracking will be sent to you via SMS or email once your order has been shipped. Please allow up to 24 hours for the tracking number to activate.

Additional duties, fees and taxes will be borne entirely by the customer. All fees, tarrifs and taxes imposed during or after shipping are the sole responsibility of the customer. Please consult your local authorities for more information on additional shipping duties before proceeding with your order.
